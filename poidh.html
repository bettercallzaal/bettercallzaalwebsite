How the boosters stack

Token Boosters - reward submitters who also hold ZABAL, SANG, ZORO, or any partner ERC-20 / NFT configured on the $ZABAL Empire. Multiplies points without changing the raw POIDH submission count.

Reputation Boosters - reward submitters above configured reputation thresholds (Talent Protocol, Phi, etc). Stacks additively with token boosters and the staking booster (capped at +5x).

How It Works

Submit. Climb. Earn ZABAL.

1

Submit to a ZAO bounty

Open a bounty in the ZAO album on POIDH, follow the proof requirement, submit your photo or clip with a Base wallet.

2

Land on the leaderboard

Your submitting wallet is indexed here automatically. Each accepted submission counts as 1 score. The feed refreshes for every new bounty in the ZAO album.

3

Earn ZABAL via Empire Builder

The $ZABAL Empire reads this leaderboard and bulk-distributes ZABAL to submitters proportional to score. No claim transaction needed - tokens land in your wallet on the next refresh.
