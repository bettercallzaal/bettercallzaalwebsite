Tier 1 - what I founded

The ZAO.

The umbrella org I founded. Started in 2022 as ZTalent Agency; pivoted to The ZAO (ZTalent Artist Organization) in 2023. Mission: bring ownership back to independent artists - profit margin, data, IP rights - rebuilt on Farcaster + Base with peer-validated governance.

Visit thezao.com Join

Why I started The ZAO

I'd watched friends release music for years and net almost nothing - 70% taken by a label, 15% taken by a streamer, 10% taken by an aggregator, the artist left with crumbs and no data on who actually listened. By 2022, the tools to flip that existed (Farcaster, Base, onchain payment rails, social graphs) but no one had stitched them together for music.

The ZAO is the stitching. Community first, governance second, tooling third. Every primitive we build - the social client (ZAO OS), the prediction markets (WaveWarZ), the festivals (ZAOstock), the token (ZABAL), the agent layer (ZOE) - serves the same thesis: artists own their margin, data, and IP.

What lives under the umbrella

For the canonical, exhaustive list see the ZABAL Nexus. Below is the working short list.

ZAO OS - the Farcaster social client for the ZAO community. Feed, music player, governance tooling. zaoos.com
WaveWarZ - onchain artist prediction markets on Solana. Live stats at wavewarz-intelligence.vercel.app.
ZAO Festivals - ZAO-Palooza (NYC, NFT NYC 2024) + ZAO-Chella (Miami, Art Basel 2024). zaofestivals.com
ZAOstock - the next ZAO festival, planned for Ellsworth, Maine. Pre-public; ops in Telegram for now.
COC Concertz - concert promotion + virtual venue community in the ecosystem. cocconcertz.com
ZOUNZ - Farcaster music mini app. AI music gen + Audius + Zora NFT on Base + attention markets on Solana.
ZABAL + Empire Builder - the ecosystem token launched via Clanker, wrapped as an Empire with leaderboards + boosters.
ZOE - the agent layer. ZAO-aware AI for ops, scheduling, onboarding, on-chain queries.
Fractal governance - peer-validated Respect-earning weekly meetings (Fractal Mondays). Governance token is earned through participation, not bought.
ZABAL Games - June prep / July submissions / August finals Farcaster vibe-coding challenge. bettercallzaal.com/zabalgames

How to plug in

If you're an artist, builder, or creator, the front door is thezao.com/about. We run weekly Fractal Monday meetings - show up consistently, share what you've worked on, earn Respect, earn governance power.

If you want to build something on top of the ZAO stack (a snap, an Empire, a leaderboard, an integration), the ZABAL Games happen June-August 2026 - prep month June, open build-a-thon July, finals August.
