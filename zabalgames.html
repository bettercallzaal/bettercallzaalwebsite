Who Decides

The ZAO DAO Votes

ZAO members who have earned Respect by contributing through fractals are the judging body. Not whoever holds the most tokens. Not a closed panel. The community that's been showing up + earning peer-validated reputation gets to pick which builds serve the ecosystem best.

How voting works

Eligible voters = ZAO members with Respect above a threshold. Respect is ZAO's soulbound peer-validated reputation - it's earned in weekly fractals over time. So the voter set naturally skews toward long-tenure contributors without needing a separate tenure rule. Voter eligibility is snapshotted at T+0 so the set is locked before the Games start. 1-person-1-vote mechanism for v0. Placement by total votes. Reveal stream at T+72h announces placements + distributes USDC + mints collectibles live onchain.

Tooling + Visibility

Use Any Harness - Show Your Work

We don't care if you use Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, Aider, Cline, Bolt, v0, Lovable, or hand-roll your own pipeline. What we care about is that you build in public. Pick at least one primary visibility mode below. Mix and match welcome.

Mode 1

Live Twitch Stream

Stream the bulk of your build live on Twitch. The default - what we recommend. ZAO restreams all 8 of you to a single hub page so viewers can switch between players.

Mode 2

Recorded Screen Sessions

Screen-record your build sessions and upload to YouTube/Loom within 1h of each session ending. Cast the link to /zabalgames as you go. Less drama than live but easier on shy builders.

Mode 3

Public AI Prompt Logs

Share your AI conversation logs publicly as you go. Cursor + Windsurf composer history, Claude Code transcript dumps, Aider session logs - whatever your tool exports. Post snippets every 1-2 hours.

Mode 4

Frequent Build Casts

Cast progress on Farcaster every 1-2 hours with screenshots, snippets, and what you're prompting. Lower bar than streaming, higher cadence required. Tag /zabalgames + @bettercallzaal.

The rule: at least one primary visibility mode, plus ongoing /zabalgames casts Voters need to verify you actually built it in 24h with AI tools - that's the only constraint. How you show your work is up to you. Pick the mode that fits your personality and ship.

FAQ

Common Questions

Who is this for? +
8 Farcasters - ideally digital creators (devs, designers, writers, video editors, musicians, streamers, podcasters, anyone who already ships in public). You don't need to be a senior engineer - Claude Code closes a lot of skill gaps. You DO need to be comfortable streaming yourself building, because the whole format is build-in-public.
What does ZAO actually do for me as a creator? +
Three things. First, distribution - ZAO accounts (BCZ Farcaster, /zao channel, COC Concertz, ZAO OS) amplify all 8 of you during the promote window so your audience grows whether you place or not. Second, infrastructure - we set up your Twitch/StreamElements onboarding, give you a pre-funded Privy wallet, and provide an Empire Builder API key. Third, content engine - the streaming flywheel auto-clips your build into shorts for TikTok/Reels/YouTube/Farcaster, so 24h of streaming becomes ~20 pieces of content you can use forever.
Do I have to use Claude Code? +
No. Use whatever vibe-coding harness you want - Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, Aider, Cline, Bolt, v0, Lovable, even hand-roll your own pipeline. The constraint is "show your work" not "use this specific tool." Pick one of the 4 visibility modes (live stream, recorded sessions, public prompt logs, or frequent build casts) and ship publicly.
Do I have to stream the entire 24h build? +
Live streaming is just one of four visibility modes. You can instead record screen sessions and upload to YouTube within 1h, share public AI prompt logs, or cast frequent screenshots every 1-2 hours. Pick what fits your personality. Required: at least one primary mode plus ongoing /zabalgames casts throughout. Voters need to verify you built it in 24h with AI tools - HOW you show your work is up to you.
Can I work as a team? +
Solo only for v0. Teams of 2 considered for v1. The point is to see what one builder can ship with Claude Code in 24 hours.
Who picks the winners? +
ZAO members who have earned Respect through fractal participation. Respect is ZAO's soulbound, peer-validated reputation - earned in weekly fractal sessions over time. The eligible voter set naturally skews toward long-tenure contributors. Voter set is snapshotted at T+0 so it's locked before the Games begin. 1-person-1-vote mechanism, NOT token-weighted (so a whale holding lots of ZABAL has no extra power - this is governance by earned contribution, not by token holdings). The people deciding are the people who've actually built ZAO and know what's worth building next.
What if my time zone is bad for PT? +
v0 is anchored to Pacific Time. We know this disadvantages EU/Asia. v1 may run regional variants. For v0: if you're up for coding through your night, you're up for it. Otherwise wait for v1.
Who owns the code I ship? +
You do. Open-source mandate (MIT or similar permissive license) is a submission requirement. ZAO doesn't claim IP. We can fork or extend per the license, but ownership stays with you.
What happens after the games? +
All 8 builds live on as open-source repos in the /zabalgames Hall of Fame. Some get organically picked up by ZAO ecosystem brands. Some die. Some you keep evolving. No obligation either way - you finished, you got the collectible, you're an alumnus.
Can I read the full spec? +
Yes - Doc 630 in the ZAO research library covers everything: format, prompt design, judging, prize, infra, risks, and strategic considerations. This page is the marketing summary; the doc is the source of truth.
