  • Testimonials and feedback: name, link, and any quote or commentary you submit through testimonial or feedback forms.
  • Billing information: business name, billing address, tax identifiers, and limited payment details necessary to invoice or be paid (handled in most cases by our payment processors, not stored by us in full).
  • Wallet and on-chain information: public blockchain wallet addresses you connect or provide in connection with any token, cryptocurrency, or digital-asset purchase or delivery.
  • Transaction information: records of purchases, invoices, and payments, including amounts, currencies, timestamps, and where applicable transaction hashes.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and timestamps.

Each third party's handling of information it collects directly from you is governed by that party's own privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as described below:
